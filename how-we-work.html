How We Work

Brewcode offers custom full-stack dev services at fair prices

Here’s a closer look at our process

We follow the Agile Methodology for product development.

Initial analysis

We want to know everything about you, we want to learn your business goals and vision. We will collect the information and identify the most important functionalities.

Design/Sprint

Once we have the software requirements in place, we design the best solution. Tools such as-…. will help us make sure the design propels you to reach your goal.

Testing

Testing is crucial. From unit testing, regression testing, and functional testing a dedicated resource will validate all the business scenarios and guarantee the requirements are accounted for with a proper traceability matrix.

Re-testing / Deployment

Once the system and the product are accepted, we will work beside you to enable you to install the product on the agreed hosting platform.

Planning and wireframing

We will explore all possibilities and deep dive into the opportunities in the industry. After identifying the key deliverables, we will create a project vision that is mutually agreed upon by your team and us.

Development

We refine the requirements based on your priorities. Functional aspects that are key for the business and process are delivered first, this makes sure that your requirements are perfectly matched.

Feedback from client

As a client, you can access the environment and are encouraged to test and interact with us throughout the development.
